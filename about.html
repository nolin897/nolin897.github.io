Born and raised in Northern New Jersey, I attended Morristown-Beard college preparatory school in Morristown, NJ. After graduating, I attended Haverford College in Haverford, PA, earning a Bachelor of Arts in Classical Studies. I fancy myself as an historian or archaeologist of ideas, committed to learning about the past and how it gave rise to our world.

My Research Interests
